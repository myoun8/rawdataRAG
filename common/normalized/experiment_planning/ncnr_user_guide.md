# Accessing the NCNR

To get access to the NCNR, you MUST register each time you visit. The required lead time and registration path depend on the purpose of your visit and your access status.

## Registration requirements by visit type

**First Time Facility User**
- See the detailed instructions at "Obtaining Access to NCNR" (https://www.nist.gov/ncnr/accessing-ncnr/obtaining-access-ncnr).
- Deadline: 35 calendar days before arrival is required, unless you are a U.S. citizen or permanent resident (with a green card), in which case a minimum of three full business days is needed.

**Returning Facility User — NOT on the NCNR Access list at the scheduled time of arrival**
- See the detailed instructions at "Obtaining Access to NCNR" (https://www.nist.gov/ncnr/accessing-ncnr/obtaining-access-ncnr).
- Deadline: 35 calendar days before arrival is required, unless you are a U.S. citizen or permanent resident (with a green card), in which case a minimum of three full business days is required. For questions regarding the 35-day requirement, contact the User Office.

**Returning Facility User — on the NCNR access list at the scheduled time of arrival**
- Request access via the NCNR-IMS system (https://ncnrims.nist.gov/).
- Deadline: three full business days in advance is needed.

**Non-User Visit (e.g., tour, talk, seminar)**
- Use the visitor registration page (https://ncnrims.nist.gov/ExternalVisitorRegistration) and fill out the request form.
- Deadline: three full business days in advance is needed.

**All other requests**
- Contact the User Office (see the live page for current User Office email and phone).

If you are unsure of your access status, contact the User Office (contact details on the live page).

## Internet Browser Requirements

Any modern browser should be able to access the IMS application. JavaScript must be enabled, so you must disable script blockers when visiting the IMS site. If you see a blank page or do not see the NCNR-IMS application, contact the User Office for further guidance.
